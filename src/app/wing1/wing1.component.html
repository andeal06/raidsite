The Forsaken Veil

Overview

The Forsaken Veil can be split into 3 seperate bosses with 1 event between the first boss Veil Guardian and Gorseval, called Spirit Run. Considered an introductory wing The Forsaken Veil introduces players to the world of raiding in Guild Wars 2. Key concepts taught in Wing 1 include; individual positioning in Veil Guardian, a hard DPS check in Gorseval*, and individual mechanic responsibilities at Sabetha.
